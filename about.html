About CCMS

Connecting Citizens with Municipal Solutions

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between citizens and municipal authorities by providing a seamless platform for reporting and resolving local issues efficiently.

Our Story

Founded with the vision of empowering citizens to actively participate in improving their communities, CCMS has been serving municipalities and residents since its inception.

Our Team

A dedicated team of developers, municipal experts, and community advocates working together to create meaningful change in local governance.

Our Impact

Over 10,000 issues resolved, 50+ municipalities served, and countless communities improved through citizen engagement and efficient problem-solving.
